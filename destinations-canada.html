Study in Canada — Exceptional Education, Exceptional Welcome.

Why Canada

Canada has rapidly become one of the most popular destinations for Indian students — and for good reason. Excellent universities, strong post-study work opportunities, a clear pathway to permanent residency, and one of the most welcoming countries in the world for international students.

What Makes It Special

  • Post-Graduate Work Permit (PGWP) for up to 3 years after graduation
  • Relatively lower tuition and living costs than the USA or UK
  • Clear immigration pathways — Express Entry, PNP programs
  • High quality of life, safe cities, multicultural society

At a Glance

Popular Programs:
Computer Science · Engineering · Business · Health Sciences · Data Analytics
Key Intakes:
September (Fall) · January (Winter) · May (Summer — limited)
Tests Required:
IELTS or TOEFL · GRE / GMAT for postgraduate programs
Typical Tuition:
CAD 20,000–40,000 per year
