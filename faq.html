General

What exactly does Apex Scholars do?
We're an end-to-end education consulting firm. We help students get into the right universities and colleges — whether that's abroad (USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and 25+ other countries) or in India (IITs, IIMs, AIIMS, NLUs, and more). We handle everything from initial counselling and university shortlisting to application support, scholarships, visa guidance, and education loans.
Who is Apex Scholars for?
Students at any stage of their education journey — Class 10 students beginning to think about their future, Class 12 students preparing for entrance exams or international applications, graduates applying for postgraduate programs, and working professionals considering executive education or career-changing degrees.
How much does Apex Scholars charge?
Our fees vary based on the services you need. Your initial counselling session is always free. We'll give you a clear breakdown of fees during that session — no hidden charges, no surprises.

Studying Abroad

When should I start planning if I want to study abroad?
The earlier, the better. For undergraduate admissions, we recommend starting in Class 11 — two full years before your intended intake. For postgraduate programs, 12–18 months before your target intake is ideal. That said, we've helped students with much shorter timelines too.
Do I need to take IELTS or TOEFL?
Most universities in the USA, UK, Canada, and Australia require an English proficiency test. Some universities waive this if you've studied in an English-medium school throughout. We'll tell you exactly what's needed for your specific shortlist.
