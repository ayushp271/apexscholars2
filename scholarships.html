Types of Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarships

Awarded based on academic excellence, test scores, and achievements. These are available at almost every university worldwide — and many go unclaimed because students don't apply.

Need-Based Scholarships

Awarded based on demonstrated financial need. Many top universities — especially in the USA — meet 100% of demonstrated financial need for admitted international students.
