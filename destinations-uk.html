Why the UK

The UK offers some of the world's most respected degrees — in just 3 years for undergraduates, saving significant time and money. Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL, and the Russell Group universities carry enormous weight with employers globally.

What Makes It Special

  • 3-year undergraduate degrees (vs. 4 in most other countries)
  • Graduate Route visa allows 2 years of post-study work
  • Strong for law, medicine, business, and the humanities
  • Rich cultural experience in one of the world's great cosmopolitan cities (London)
