Study in Germany — World-Class Engineering. Often Zero Tuition.

Why Germany

Germany is one of the world's best-kept secrets in international education. Public universities charge little to no tuition, even for international students. The country is a global leader in engineering, technology, and the sciences — and its universities are consistently ranked among the world's best.

What Makes It Special

  • Tuition-free or very low tuition at public universities
  • Strong in engineering, computer science, automotive, and manufacturing sectors
  • Post-study work visa for 18 months to seek employment
  • Growing number of English-taught programs at master's level
  • Central European location — ideal for travel and cultural immersion

At a Glance

Popular Programs:
Mechanical Engineering · Automotive Engineering · Computer Science · MBA · Architecture · Physics
Key Intakes:
October (Winter Semester) · April (Summer Semester)
Tests Required:
IELTS or TOEFL for English-taught programs
Typical Tuition:
€0–3,000 per semester (public universities) · €10,000–25,000 per year (private universities)
