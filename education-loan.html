Secured Loans (with collateral)

Lower interest rates. Require collateral such as property or fixed deposits. Typically offered by Indian nationalised banks.

Lenders: SBI Global Ed-Vantage · Bank of Baroda · Punjab National Bank

Unsecured Loans (without collateral)

No collateral required. Slightly higher interest rates. Faster processing. Ideal for students without assets to pledge.

Lenders: HDFC Credila · Avanse · InCred · Auxilo
